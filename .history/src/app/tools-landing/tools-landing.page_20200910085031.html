The V believes in continuous progress, and this can only be attained if opportunities for advancement are provided for our V Ambassadors. As shown in the diagram below, an Independent Representative can attain V Elite Leader status once he or she has completed the V Advancement Programs successfully. Once they gain membership in V Elite Leaders’ pool, they are now eligible to be nominated into the V Council.

From this point forward, any upward movement will be dependent on nomination and performance evaluation. Each level up requires a much tougher and stringent evaluation process, and also means that more responsibility is being undertaken by a V Ambassador. Benefits and privileges of each level of V Ambassadorship will be discussed in detail in the following chapters.

MDF GUIDELINES

(Market Development Fund)

The MDF is a service fund that applies only to V Partners, Associate V Partners and V Council members. It functions as assistance, NOT as an entitlement. This fund may be acquired based on proposed Market Development Activities such as Business Presentations (BP), one-onone presentations, speaking/training engagements, Network Development Activities (NDA) and/or Networking Seminar Series (NSS) and other VEvents.

This assistance may be revoked and revised at any time by The V, especially when the company’s expectations are not met. V Ambassadors are encouraged to utilize their MDF or fulfill any network building/management activities every month.
